About Us

A little dessert bar with big flavors

The Sweet Bar lives in Troutdale, right by the gateway to the Columbia River Gorge. We whip up crispy bubble waffles, creamy hand-scooped ice cream, over-the-top milkshakes and refreshing fruit sodas — all made fresh when you order.

Come grab a treat after a hike, on a sunny stroll through historic Troutdale, or just because it's Tuesday. Sweet moments are always in season.
